# Report Contract

`recommendation.md` must include:

- scope and inspected inputs
- delivery status
- a journal list in all cases, including non-ready runs
- fit band for each venue
- estimated acceptance-chance interval for each venue if submitted as-is
- confidence, calculation class, base-rate interval, modifier intervals, and
  caveats for every acceptance-chance estimate
- comparator evidence matrix with provider, source/query IDs, year window,
  evidence level, and evidence IDs
- per-venue evidence summary
- ordinal scorecard components with caveats
- unsupported or stale evidence gaps
- Review Findings
- Delivery Check

Separate observations from inferred fit. Include `incomplete analysis` when
material evidence is missing or provider coverage is blocked.

`incomplete analysis` must appear near the top of the report when material
evidence is missing. Bibliography overlap, reputation, metadata-only comparator
records, and offline placeholders are discovery signals only and must not be
presented as a deliverable ranked shortlist.

Acceptance-chance language is allowed only as a heuristic interval with
calculation details. Reports must not say or imply that the tool predicts,
guarantees, or knows the actual acceptance outcome.

Do not include raw draft text by default.
