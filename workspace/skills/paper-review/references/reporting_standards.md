# Reporting Standards Quick Reference

Check the study type before applying a checklist.

## Common standards

- `CONSORT`: randomized trials
- `STROBE`: observational studies
- `PRISMA`: systematic reviews and meta-analyses
- `ARRIVE`: animal experiments
- `CARE`: case reports
- `MIAME` / `MINSEQE`: microarray and sequencing studies

## What to verify

- study design is stated clearly
- inclusion and exclusion criteria are explicit
- sample size justification exists when expected
- randomization, blinding, and missing-data handling are described when relevant
- statistical methods match the design
- data, code, accession, or deposition identifiers are included when expected
- limitations are discussed honestly
